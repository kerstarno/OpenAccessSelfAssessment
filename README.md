# ORLA - Open Resource & Learning Assistant
ORLA consists of two parts:
* An Open Access Self-Assessment Tool that provides you with an evaluation of where you stand on your journey of making your cultural collections available with open access, plus indicating what might be suitable first/next steps for you to take.
* The Open Resource & Learning Assistant that can point you to useful resources, tools, experts, services that might help you take these first/next steps.

Both are the result of a working group activity during the Oulu Löyly 2026, held between 8 and 10 June 2026 in Oulu, Finland. As such the current version should be considered a prototype and proof-of-concept. Find out more about the [work process](https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Oulu_L%C3%B6yly/Documentation/ORLA_-_Open_Resource_%26_Learning_Assistant) that led to ORLA.
